Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our beauty line, delivery, or return policies? Find quick answers right here.

Yes, absolutely! All Ziva Cosmetics products are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and organic. We do not test on animals, and we only source ethically manufactured, clean-beauty ingredients certified by organic standards.

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below this threshold, a small shipping fee of ₹40 applies. Deliveries typically take 3 to 5 business days depending on your location (metro cities usually receive orders within 48 hours).

We have a hassle-free 15-day return and exchange policy. If you receive a damaged product or if a shade does not suit you, you can initiate a return request from your dashboard or contact our customer support team at support@ziva.com. The product must be unused and in its original packaging.

We accept a wide variety of payment options, including Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit Cards, Debit Cards, and Net Banking. All transactions are routed through fully secured PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.
